Game Rules

The game enroles two players on one and only screen. Each player has a temporary score (ROUND) and a global score (GLOBAL). On player's round, the round score of the player is set to zero, then the player can roll the die as many time he wants. The result of round is added to ROUND score of the player. During his turn, the player can decide to :

  • Click on HOLD button, so that his current round score is added to his GLOBAL score. As a result, the second player take hand.
  • Roll the die. If he get a ONE, he lose his current round score and let the second player play.
    • The winner is the first player to obtain 100 points with his global score.
